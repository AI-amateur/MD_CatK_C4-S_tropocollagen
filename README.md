Exploration of Tropocollagen Unwinding by Cathepsin K Dimer with Chondroitin 4-sulfate through Microsecond Timescale Molecular Dynamics

The text describes the molecular dynamics (MD) simulation trajectories and movies files included in the supplementary materials. 

==================================================

Trajectory Folder

The section lists the trajectory files for each MD simulation, organized by number in the main text. 
Note：Duplicated simulations (simulation1 and simulation2) were carried out for each system with different random seeds to ensure reproducibility.

1. File:  0_tropocollagen

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the tropocollagen molecule. 

*Simulation Time: 2 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 400 ns.
*Data Source:
    simulation1 is the first replicate simulation.
    simulation2 is the second replicate simulation.

1. File:  1_tropocollagen_chondroitin

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the tropocollagen + C4-S complex. 

*Simulation Time: 1 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 200 ns.
*Data Source:
    simulation1 corresponds to Fig. S4 in the supplementary materials.
    simulation2 is the second replicate simulation.

Note：tropocollagen_chondroitin_FEL.pdb is the minimum energy conformation of tropocollagen + C4-S used to build the CatK dimer + tropocollagen + C4-S complex.

2. File:  2_CatKdimer_tropocollagen_chondroitin

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the CatK dimer + tropocollagen + C4-S complex.

*Simulation Time: 2 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 400 ns.
*Data Source:
    simulation1 corresponds to Fig. 5 in the main text.
    simulation2 corresponds to Fig. S8 in the supplementary materials.

3. File: 3_CatKdimer_tropocollagen

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the CatK dimer + tropocollagen complex.

*Simulation Time: 1 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 200 ns.
*Data Source:
     simulation1 corresponds to Fig. S12 in the supplementary materials.
     simulation2 is the second replicate simulation.

4. File:  4_CatKdimer_mutation_Cys25

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the mutant CatKB (Cys→Ala at residue 25) + CatKA + tropocollagen + C4-S complex.

*Simulation Time: 1 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 200 ns.
*Data Source:
    simulation1 corresponds to Fig. 9(A) in the main text.
    simulation2 is the second replicate simulation.

5. File:  5_CatKdimer_mutation_Trp184

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the mutant CatKB (Trp→Ala at residue 184) + CatKA +tropocollagen+ C4-S complex.

*Simulation Time: 1 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 200 ns.
*Data Source:
    simulation1 corresponds to Fig. 9(B) in the main text.
    simulation2 is the second replicate simulation.

6. File: 6_CatKB_tropocollagen_chondroitin

System: Time-resolved structural evolution of the CatKB + tropocollagen + C4-S complex.

*Simulation Time: 1 μs.
*Snapshot Interval: 200 ns.
*Data Source:
      simulation1 corresponds to Fig. S16 in the supplementary materials.
      simulation2 is the second replicate simulation.

Movie Folder
